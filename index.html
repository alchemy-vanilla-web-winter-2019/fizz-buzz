Fizz*Buzz

Test your favorite number!

Test whether your favorite number is divisible by 3, 5, or both! I'll count up to your number, noting which numbers are divisible by 3, 5, or both along the way.

  • Divisible by 3 = fizz
  • Divisible by 5 = buzz
  • Divisible by both 3 and 5 = fizzbuzz
